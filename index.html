Frequently asked questions

If you are unsure or wondering if consolidating all your account systems
into one dashboard is good for you. Do not worry, we are here to answer
all your queries.

What is FinFast?+

  • FinFast is a platform that allows business owners manage their invoicing, track expences, track sales, keep financial records and customers' data and to manage stock.

What makes FinFast different from other Accounting softwares?+

  • FinFast is designed to streamline the user's accounting process, by integrating all functional tools into a single and user-friendly interface.

Can I access FinFast on my mobile phone?+

  • Yes. FinFast can be accessed from any web enabled mobile device with an internet browser. The experience is optimised on any iOS device and Andriod tablet or Smartphone with the FinFast Online App.

Is my information safe on FinFast?+

  • Absolutely! FinFast operates a non-disclosure policy, thereby making all information of every user safe and private.

What should I do if I need more help?+

  • Please contact us by clicking the 'Contact' button on the landing page or you can contact customer support by sending an email to finfastcrm@zulu.com.
